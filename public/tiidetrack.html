TiideTrack

An attendance tracking website

A website that provides a simple, secure, and convenient way for users to manage and pay for essential services, including electricity bills, cable subscriptions, and data plans. Designed for ease of use, it allows users to quickly access multiple services in one place, view their account details, track usage, and make payments.

Key Features

  • Service Selection and Management
  • Payment Options and Wallet Integration
  • Real-Time Billing Information
  • Usage History and Reports
  • Transaction History and Receipts
